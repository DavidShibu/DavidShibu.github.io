Real-Time Voice Scam Detector using BERT

Overview Link to heading

This project addresses the rise in “vishing” (voice phishing) by providing users with an immediate risk assessment of their phone conversations. The system monitors the call transcript and, upon termination of the call, instantly notifies the user if the conversation exhibited patterns typical of fraudulent activity.

My Role Link to heading

  • Integrated OpenAI Whisper for high-accuracy, real-time speech-to-text transcription.
  • Fine-tuned a BERT-base-uncased model on a custom dataset of scam scripts and legitimate business conversations to identify “urgency” and “authority” triggers.
  • Developed a post-call trigger logic that processes the accumulated transcript the moment the user hangs up.
  • Built the notification interface to display a “Scam” vs. “Safe” probability score.

Impact Link to heading

  • Context-Aware Security: Unlike simple keyword blockers, the BERT-based approach understands the intent behind the words, making it resistant to scammers who change their vocabulary.
  • Immediate Feedback Loop: By providing the analysis the moment the call ends, it prevents users from following through on fraudulent instructions (like making a payment or sharing a PIN) given during the call.
  • Privacy-First Design: Focused on local transcription and inference to ensure sensitive conversation data remains secure.
