About

I am David Shibu, a Computer Science undergraduate at Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham and a dual-degree student pursuing a B.S. in Data Science and Applications from IIT Madras (off-campus). I have a strong interest in building reliable, real-world software systems and exploring data-driven solutions to complex problems.

I enjoy working at the intersection of programming, mathematics, and analytical thinking. My academic and project experience spans software engineering, web development, IoT systems, and applied machine learning. I am particularly drawn to algorithmic problem-solving, competitive programming, and areas related to quantitative analysis and computing, where logical rigor and efficiency matter most.

Through national-level hackathons, research work, and team-led projects, I have developed the ability to perform effectively under pressure, manage time efficiently, and take ownership of tasks from ideation to execution. I value collaboration and actively contribute to the communities and teams I am part of by sharing knowledge and supporting peers whenever possible.

I am continuously motivated to learn, adapt, and improve — both technically and personally — while contributing meaningfully to projects that create real impact.
